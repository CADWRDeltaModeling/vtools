Introduction¶

Vtools is a package to perform time-aligned operations on time series, as well as some specialty analyses encountered in hydrology and hydrodynamic work and modeling. The goal of vtools is to fill in around tools like pandas and netcdf4-python and to provide a bridge between netcdf and the legacy format HEC-DSS from the Hydrologic Engineering Center of the Army Corps of Engineers.
